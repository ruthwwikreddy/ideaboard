# üîç SEO & Google Indexing Guide for IdeaBoard

This guide will help you get your IdeaBoard website indexed and appearing in Google search results.

## ‚ö° Immediate Actions (Do These First)

### 1. **Submit Your Site to Google Search Console**

// turbo-all

**Step 1.1:** Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
   - Sign in with your Google account
   - Click "Add Property"
   - Enter your domain: `https://www.ideaboard.ai`
   - Verify ownership (use HTML tag method recommended)

**Step 1.2:** Add your sitemap
   - In Search Console, go to "Sitemaps" (left sidebar)
   - Submit: `https://www.ideaboard.ai/sitemap.xml`
   - This tells Google exactly which pages to index

**Step 1.3:** Request Indexing for Key Pages
   - Go to "URL Inspection" tool
   - Enter URLs one by one:
     - `https://www.ideaboard.ai`
     - `https://www.ideaboard.ai/pricing`
     - `https://www.ideaboard.ai/about-us`
   - Click "Request Indexing" for each
   - This speeds up the indexing process significantly

### 2. **Submit to Bing Webmaster Tools**

**Step 2.1:** Go to [Bing Webmaster Tools](https://www.bing.com/webmasters)
   - Sign in with Microsoft account
   - Add your site: `https://www.ideaboard.ai`
   - Import from Google Search Console (easier)
   - Submit your sitemap

**Why Bing?** Bing powers Yahoo, DuckDuckGo, and other search engines. Plus, Bing often indexes faster than Google.

### 3. **Create a Google Business Profile**

**Step 3.1:** Go to [Google Business Profile](https://business.google.com)
   - Create a business profile for IdeaBoard
   - Add your website URL
   - Add business category: "Software Company" or "Technology Company"
   - This helps with local and brand searches
